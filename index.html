Welcome to Midnight is a campaign that TWLOHA runs every December to encourage those to stay even when it's hard. This year I'm running a charity livestream from 7pm to 1am! My fundraising goal is reflective of next year with a goal of $2,021.00 ~ a fun little number stream. I will be live December 31st and all games played will be open for group participation! Expect games like Among Us, Various Jackbox Party games, and more. I hope to see you midnight and that you choose to stay with me in 2021.

To Write Love on Her Arms is a nonprofit movement dedicated to presenting hope and finding help for people struggling with depression, addiction, self-injury, and suicide. TWLOHA exists to encourage, inform, inspire, and invest directly into treatment and recovery.
